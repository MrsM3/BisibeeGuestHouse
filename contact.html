Directions

From George: At the third set of traffic lights, turn left into Church St. Cross the river and then at the second 4-way stop you'll see Bisibee Guesthouse on the left. From De Rust: At the third set of traffic lights, turn right into Baron van Reede. At the second set of traffic lights, turn left into Church St. Cross the river and then at the second 4-way stop you'll see Bisibee Guesthouse on the left. From Cape Town & Mossel Bay: At the first set of traffic lights, turn left into Jan van Riebeeck. At the second stop street, turn left into Church St. At the next stop you'll see Bisibee Guesthouse on the left. GPS: S 33 15 14.4 E 22 11 33.7
