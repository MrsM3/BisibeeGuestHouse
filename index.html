Welcome...

Bisibee Guest House offers affordable accommodation in Oudtshoorn. It is situated in a well established area of Oudtshoorn and is within walking distance of the town centre.

Facilities available on premises include: swimming pool, outdoor seating and free WiFi access. Each room has tea/coffee facilities, small fridge and SABC TV.

Bisibee provides a very convenient stop-over for people travelling between Cape Town, Pretoria, Bloemfontein, Beaufort West, Port Elizabeth and Addo Elephant Park. Oudtshoorn forms part of the Klein Karoo and acts as a portal for the well renowned Swartberg Pass, The Hell and Great Karoo as well as the Garden Route along the R62.

A nice day can be spent driving over the Swartberg Pass via Prince Albert Hamlet and then back through Meiringspoort Pass and De Rust, before returning to Oudtshoorn.

Locally, one can visit the Rust en Vrede Waterfall, Cango Caves, Cango Wildlife Ranch and Ostrich Farms.

Bisibee has sufficient secure parking and a well established garden where one can relax after a day of touring or business.
